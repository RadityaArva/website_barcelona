fc barcelona

Known as “Barça,” FC Barcelona is one of the most successful and iconic soccer clubs in the the world. Founded in 1899 in the Spanish city of Barcelona, the club has a rich history of achievements, both at national and international levels. With a philosophy of attacking play and a commitment to youth development through its La Masia academy, Barça has produced many legendary players such as Lionel Messi, Xavi and Andrés Iniesta.
